Frequently Asked Questions

What are the system requirements?

Instancify requires iOS 16.0 or later, and an active AWS account with EC2 access.

How secure is my AWS credential storage?

We use iOS Keychain for secure credential storage. Your AWS credentials never leave your device and are not stored on any servers.

Does Instancify support all AWS regions?

Yes, Instancify supports all AWS regions where EC2 services are available.
